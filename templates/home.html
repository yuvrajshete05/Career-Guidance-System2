AI-Generated Resume Tips

Tailor Your Resume to the Job

Our AI suggests customizing your resume for each application. Highlight keywords from the job description and showcase skills most relevant to the role you're targeting. This significantly increases your chances of passing applicant tracking systems (ATS).

Quantify Your Achievements

Instead of just listing responsibilities, use numbers and metrics to demonstrate your impact. For example, "Increased sales by 15%" or "Managed a team of 10+" is far more impactful than vague statements.

Optimize for Keywords

Based on your chosen career, our AI identifies crucial industry-specific keywords. Integrate these naturally throughout your resume, especially in your skills section and work experience bullet points, to align with employer searches.

Choose a Clean, Readable Format

Clarity is key. Opt for a professional, uncluttered layout that's easy to scan. Use clear headings, consistent formatting, and appropriate white space. Avoid overly flashy designs that might distract from your content.

Highlight Relevant Skills

Beyond technical skills, emphasize soft skills like problem-solving, communication, and teamwork that are highly valued in your desired career. Use action verbs to describe how you applied these skills in previous roles.

Proofread Meticulously

Even with AI assistance, a final human review is crucial. Check for any typos, grammatical errors, or inconsistencies. A flawless resume reflects attention to detail and professionalism. Consider using online tools for a final check.
